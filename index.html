Rise of the Phoenix!

A long long time ago in a galaxy far far away…

Turmoil has engulfed the property data industry in England and Wales. The citizens' homes and their good night's sleep are in dispute.

Struggling to survive the high-rising skyscrapers and new developments,
conveyancing search providers have started drowning in paperwork in the small planet of Title Enquiries.

While the Land Registry endlessly debates this alarming chain of events, Phoenix has risen to transform the property data industry with ceaseless cooperation between the Acumen and Geeks teams, the guardians of innovation and intelligence in the market, to settle the conflict.

The long story, like all of those with Micky, starts in the form of unrest in Morden, South London, where Andrew and Somayeh have declared their intentions to the rest of the world…
